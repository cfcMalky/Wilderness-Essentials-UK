Campfire Safety & Leave No Trace Principles

Campfires are a highlight of camping—but only if done safely and responsibly.

  • UK Fire Laws: Where fires are allowed, when a stove is better.
  • Building a Safe Fire: Fire pits/rings, clear debris, keep water nearby.
  • Burning Responsibly: Only dry wood; never burn rubbish or plastics.
  • Leave No Trace: Douse, scatter ashes, restore the area.
  • Alternatives: Use a portable stove—safer and often required.
