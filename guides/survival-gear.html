Essential Survival Gear for UK Adventures

A basic survival kit can be a lifesaver in the wild.

  • Navigation: OS map, compass, GPS (with spare power).
  • First Aid: Plasters, bandages, wipes, blister treatment, painkillers, tick remover.
  • Emergency Shelter: Foil blanket, bivvy bag, tarp.
  • Signalling: Whistle, mirror, torch.
  • Fire Starting: Waterproof matches, fire steel, tinder.
  • Cutting Tools: Multi-tool or small knife (legal carry in UK).
  • Other: Duct tape, cord, safety pins, medication.
