The Ultimate Camping Checklist for Beginners (UK Edition)

Don’t get caught out by the weather or forget a crucial piece of kit! This printable checklist covers all the essentials for a safe, comfortable trip in the UK.

  • Shelter & Sleeping: Tent, groundsheet, sleeping bag (3-season), sleeping mat/air bed, pillow, mallet.
  • Clothing: Waterproof jacket, fleece, base layers, hiking boots, extra socks, hat, gloves, quick-dry trousers.
  • Cooking & Food: Camping stove, fuel, lighter, pans, utensils, plates, mugs, sponge, food, snacks.
  • Lighting & Power: Headtorch/lantern, spare batteries, power bank, charger.
  • Safety & Navigation: First aid kit, map, compass, whistle, emergency blanket.
  • Personal Items: Toothbrush, biodegradable wipes, toilet paper, sun cream, insect repellent.
  • Extras: Camp chairs, table, tarp, bin bags, water bottle, multi-tool, duct tape.
  • For UK Weather: Pack for rain, mud, and chilly nights—even in summer!
