Wild Camping in the UK: Law, Ethics, and Best Spots

Wild camping is an incredible way to experience the UK’s wild places—if you do it legally and respectfully.

  • Where It’s Legal: Scotland (freedom to roam), Dartmoor (some areas), elsewhere with permission.
  • Leave No Trace: Pitch late, leave early, no fires, pack out waste.
  • Best Practice: Small discreet tents, no large groups, silent after dark.
  • Top Spots: Scottish Highlands, Lake District, Dartmoor, parts of Wales (ask landowners).
  • Staying Safe: Weather checks, emergency plans, GPX for navigation.
