Choosing and Using Sleeping Bags & Mats for UK Camping

A good night’s sleep is key to an enjoyable camp. Here’s how to pick sleeping bags and mats that suit UK conditions.

  • Bag Types: Mummy vs. rectangular, down vs. synthetic.
  • Temperature Ratings: Comfort vs. extreme; 3-season for most UK camping.
  • Size & Fit: Length and width for your body type.
  • Sleeping Mats: Inflatable vs. foam, R-values, weight for backpacking.
  • Caring for Gear: Storage, cleaning, quick-drying tips.
