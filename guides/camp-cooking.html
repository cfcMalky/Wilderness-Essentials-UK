Backpacking Food Ideas & Camp Cooking Tips

Fuel your adventure with easy, delicious, and lightweight meals.

  • Meal Planning: Balance carbs, protein, fat; lightweight, high-energy foods.
  • Breakfast: Oats, granola, dried fruit, boil-in-the-bag.
  • Lunch: Wraps, hard cheese, cured meats, nuts, snack bars.
  • Dinner: Dehydrated meals, pasta, rice, couscous, tinned fish.
  • Snacks: Trail mix, chocolate, flapjacks, energy gels.
  • Cooking Gear: Lightweight stoves, pans, sporks, collapsible bowls.
  • Water Safety: Filters, purifiers, finding safe water in the UK.
