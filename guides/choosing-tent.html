How to Choose the Right Tent for UK Weather

The British climate is unpredictable—here’s how to pick a tent that keeps you dry and comfortable.

  • Types: Dome, tunnel, geodesic, pop-up—pros & cons for UK use.
  • Waterproof Ratings: Look for at least 2000mm hydrostatic head, taped seams, quality groundsheet.
  • Weather Resistance: Sturdy poles, good guy lines, robust zips, low profiles for wind.
  • Ventilation: Vents, mesh, double-skin to prevent condensation.
  • Size & Weight: Group size, headroom, backpacking vs car camping.
  • Top UK Brands: Vango, Berghaus, Coleman, Outwell, and more.
